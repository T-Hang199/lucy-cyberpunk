Cyberpunk 2077Lucy

Lucyna Kushinada, more commonly known as just Lucy, is the deuteragonist of Cyberpunk: Edgerunners. Lucy is a young woman of slender build with light skin. She has naturally white hair and red-purple eyes. She wears light red eye and lip makeup. She is a member of the Aldecaldos, a nomad clan that resides in the Badlands outside of Night City. Lucy is a skilled netrunner, capable of hacking into the most secure systems. She is also a talented engineer, able to repair and modify complex machinery. Lucy is a kind and caring person, always willing to help those in need.
